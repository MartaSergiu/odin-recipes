Lasagna recipe

Description

Lets see what it take to make the most simple and delicius lasagna bolognese, with homemade bolognese and bechamel sauce.

For ingredients and instructions see below steps.

  • Minced meat(beef and pork mix): 500g
  • Onions: 2 large onions
  • Tomatoes: 500g
  • Milk: 500ml
  • Flour: 2 spoons
  • Butter: 100g
  • Lasagna pasta: 1 package
  • Italian condiments
  1. With all ingredients bought, boil water and add you lasanga pasta.
  2. In a separate pot add finely chopped onions, after nice and brown add minced meat and sear.
  3. When golden brown add tomatoes, basil, condiments and simmer over small heat.
  4. While simmering in a sperate pot make you bechame sauce. Add a knob of butter.
  5. After it melted add a spoon of flour, cook for 1 minute and add milk slowly while stiring without stop.
  6. Assemble: lasagna pasta, bolognese sauce, bechamel sauce, repeat until finished. Then bake until golden bronw on top.
